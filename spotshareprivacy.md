SpotShare Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 July 2026

Privacy Policy

SpotShare (“the App”) is developed and maintained by FDL (“we”, “our”, or “us”). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled responsibly.

This Privacy Policy explains how SpotShare collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

Depending on how your organization has configured SpotShare, we may collect the following information:

* Name
* Work email address
* Employee or user identifier
* Authentication information provided through your organization’s identity provider
* Parking reservations and booking history
* Parking bay release activity
* Device information required for application performance and security
* Diagnostic and crash information (where enabled)

SpotShare does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information unless required by your organization.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

* Authenticate your identity
* Enable parking bay reservations and releases
* Display your current and future bookings
* Manage parking availability
* Improve application performance and reliability
* Respond to support requests
* Maintain application security and prevent misuse

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Your information may be shared only:

* With your employer or organization administering SpotShare
* With trusted service providers who host or support the application
* Where required by law or legal process

Any third-party service providers are expected to protect your information using appropriate security measures.

Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Although we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to:

* Provide the SpotShare service
* Meet legal or contractual obligations
* Support operational and security requirements

Information is securely deleted or anonymized when it is no longer required.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction and your organization’s policies, you may have the right to:

* Request access to your personal information
* Request correction of inaccurate information
* Request deletion of personal information where applicable
* Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

Requests should be submitted through your organization’s administrator or by contacting us.

Children’s Privacy

SpotShare is intended for workplace use and is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at the published Privacy Policy URL.
